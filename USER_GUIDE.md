## üèòÔ∏è 2. For Residents (Homeowners/Citizens)
This is the core of the app. Your goal is to manage waste and earn rewards.

### **The Dashboard**
- **Express Tracking Card:** Clicking the blue **"Express"** card opens a live map showing where the collection truck is right now.
- **Kg Collected:** View how much total waste you've diverted from landfills.

### **Booking a Pickup (Two Ways)**
Click the **"Book"** button in Quick Actions:
1.  **Pre-book (Scheduled):** Use this to plan ahead. Pick a Date and Time that suits you.
2.  **Instant (Release Now):** Use this if you see the truck is already in your ward. This skips the time/date selection so you can alert the worker immediately.

### **Earning Rewards**
- Every time you dispose of waste properly, you earn **Green Points**.
- Check the **Rewards** tab in the bottom navigation to see your balance and available eco-friendly rewards.

## üí° 6. App Tips & Best Practices
- **Waste Segregation:** Always mark the correct waste type (Dry, Wet, Organic, E-Waste) when booking to ensure proper processing.
- **Live Maps:** Check the **Express** card before booking to see if a truck is already near your street for an "Instant Release".
- **Notifications:** Keep notifications ON to get alerts when the truck is 2 minutes away from your house.
